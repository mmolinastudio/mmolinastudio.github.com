IPTool - OHIM Backoffice

IPTool is an ambitious project, born with the aim of being the renewed Backoffice Application for OHIM. It will be used by the funtionaries of the office for registering Trademarks and Designs inside the EU.

My role here was to design and develop the UI as part of a team of 3 Front-End Developers. It was being developed entirely with Backbone and libraries like Require, jQuery or Underscore, and following scrum principles.

The project was going fine, meeting the dealines and tests, but for economical reasons and probably other political reasons, the project was frozen indefinitely in march.
